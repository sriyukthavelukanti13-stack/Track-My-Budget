Your guide to getting started and troubleshooting on Track My Budget

How to Download Your PhonePe Mini Statement



Step 1: Open PhonePe and Navigate to History

Launch the PhonePe app on your phone and tap on the History section on the home screen.

Step 2: Access Downloading the Statement

Tap the My Statement option, typically found in the top-right corner of the History screen.

Step 3: Select 'Mini Statement'

Choose the 'Mini Statement' duration option and click on download from the main dashboard to proceed.

Step 4: Access Mini Statement

After downloading it, tap on the ‘ View Mini Statement’ option to view your latest transactions.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with budgeting on this platform?

To get started, register and log in. Then add your income and expenses manually or by uploading your mini statement. Our tools help you visualize your spending and set budgets.

How do I add my income and expenses to the platform?

You can add transactions manually using the entry form or upload your mini statement PDF to import your data automatically.

Can I link my bank account to automatically track my expenses?

Currently, our platform supports uploads via PhonePe mini statements. Bank account linking is planned for a future release.

How do I set a monthly budget limit for different categories?

After logging in, use the Budget section on your dashboard to set your overall monthly budget. In future releases, you can allocate budgets to individual spending categories.

What should I do if my login fails?

Check that you entered the correct username and password, and make sure you've registered. If the issue persists, try resetting your password.

How can I generate graphs from my transactions?

Once you log in and upload your transactions, go to the Graph page. There you can view different charts (e.g., daily trends, category breakdown, etc.) based on your data.
